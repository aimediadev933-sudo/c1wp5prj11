Project Overview

Regional Collaboration

An ambitious effort to strengthen ecological monitoring capabilities through acoustic technologies across key Asian countries, fostering cross-border data sharing and collaborative biodiversity research.

Technology Integration

Deploying diverse networks of terrestrial, ultrasonic, and aquatic sound sensors for long-term, non-invasive environmental monitoring and species vocalization analysis.

Key Outcomes

Acoustic Toolkits

Developed 3 specialized tools: long-term spectrogram, chorus detector, and event classifier for comprehensive soundscape analysis.

Capacity Building

Conducted 6 workshops (3 data collection, 3 analysis) engaging over 100 researchers and technicians from 10+ countries.

Case Studies

Three distinct ecosystem studies covering forest, bat, and marine environments, demonstrating versatile applications.

Open Science Platform

Launched public data repositories and visualization interfaces supporting open science and collaborative research.
